BIVA, a Bengali word with the meaning beam of light, an organization revealing light of development for the deprived, has come into being.

Year of Establishment: BIVA was established in 1995.

Vision:

To establish a society democratic, free of exploitation and poverty.

Mission:

Ensure to life with dignity, enhance better living standard of the poorer people through developing their access and active participation and through providing proper Education, Health facilities, creation of Employment opportunities, Social security, establish Good governance and Gender Equity.

Goal:

The goal of the organization is to improve living standard and socio-economic condition of the under privileged people through eliminating disparity and poverty.

Objectives:
